About the Federation

The Guardians of Armenian Strength

Our History

The Armenian Mas-Wrestling Federation was founded in 2011. It is a full member of the International Mas-Wrestling Federation. The federation organizes national championships, trains coaches, and represents Armenia in European and World Championships.

What is Mas-Wrestling?

Mas-Wrestling is a national sport of Yakutia. Two athletes sit facing each other, feet on a board, and pull a wooden stick. The winner pulls the stick away or pulls the opponent.

"Very similar to the ancient Armenian game 'Paytk Kashotsi'."

The key to victory: strength, speed, agility and quick thinking.
